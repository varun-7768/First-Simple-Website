The president of the institution Sri. S. Prasada Raju carries forward the vision of his father Late Sri Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju , a philanthropist, driving the institution on the academic path of excellence. The college is replete with lush coconut trees spanning across a plot area of 30 acres at China Amiram, Bhimavaram, on Bhimavaram-Vijayawada state highway and it is also well-connected to all other places by road and rail transport. The college offers clusters of elegant and capacious buildings with state-of-the-art laboratories, class rooms, seminar halls and other amenities coupled with eco-friendly ambience on the campus for efficacious pedagogical practices.
